The city that never sleeps at night, NYC, is where tourists want to go to experience all the hype. Choosing where you are going to stay is an important part of that experience! Our goal in the data analysis was to determine the perfect location and price for your Airbnb rental. Through various different factors, we were able to determine the most ideal places to have that ultimate NYC experience. Through exploring factors such as price, the number of stays, the neighborhood, certain words in listings we were able to pinpoint the busiest, most popular places to stay in the city.

Do you ever wonder how you are going to find that perfect place to rent? Well make sure you are considering more than just the reviews and what the place looks like. We are here to show you that there are many other factors that you should take into consideration when you are booking your next Airbnb NYC trip!

I think we can all agree that price is a very important factor when it comes to deciding where you want to stay for your trip. The correlation between price and the location of the Airbnb you are looking to stay in are important. There is definitely data to back that one up. As you can imagine, the more expensive Airbnb is, the “better” the neighborhood it is located in. And the other way around as well, the cheaper the Airbnb the “worse” the location it is in.

As you can see through this graph, we have Manhattan with the highest prices and the Bronx with the cheapest prices. Not only does Manhattan average the highest priced airbnbs, but it is also the most populated with Airbnb rentals. We can conclude that the top three most expensive boroughs to stay in are Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Staten Island. But, the Airbnbs that have the most people staying in that area are Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens. So, with that being said, there is no correlation between the average price and the amount of stays in the boroughs.

The neighborhood/borough is definitely a huge decision maker. You want to be in the heart of the city, or as close as you can to it, or at least that is what our data has shown to prove. Taking a look at the data we have found, that place to be would be Manhattan. We can see that people are swayed by much more than just where the place is and what the place looks like.

Another thing that you might want to consider when deciding which Airbnb is the one for you, is the space you are being provided. If you take a close look, some Airbnbs do not offer the entire apartment for you to rent out. In some cases you will only be renting out a private room and in others you might only be getting part of the room to share with someone else.

As you can imagine you will be paying more for the entire apartment, but by how much? Well if you take a look at this graph, you can see that it is in fact, a decent price difference. Overall, the shared room type had an average price of $70.13, while the private room had an average price of $89.78, and the entire home/apartment had an average price of $211.79. The average price of the entire home/apt increased significantly from the private room. This is likely due to the vast increase of privacy that is presented with the room upgrade. I would argue that this room upgrade is definitely worth the price in this situation, what do you think?

Ever wonder where the most expensive Airbnbs in NYC are located? Well, here it is anyway. By narrowing it down and taking a look into the top 40 most expensive, every single one is located in Manhattan and Brooklyn, with the exception of one being in Staten Island and another in the Bronx, both towards the bottom of that list.

We can conclude that Manhattan and Brooklyn must have something the rest of the boroughs do not, considering they are the top most expensive and also have the most traffic through Airbnbs. You can also see that the majority of these top 40 most expensive Airbnbs are the entire apartment. But, what is interesting is there are a few that are only private rooms. Out of the 40, 6 of those Airbnbs are only renting out a private room, there must be some nice private rooms there renting out to make it on the top 40!

Ever looking for that perfect place to stay and one of the listings catches your eye with just one word? Well, whether you realize it or not, the language used to describe the Airbnb listings has more of an effect than you knew. The words used to describe rental Airbnb properties have an effect on your decision and the price of the place as well.

This graph confirms that there is a correlation between the words used in Airbnb listings and the price of the place. People are influenced not only by what the place looks like, but also by the way the place is being described. This graph above shows that words such as “cozy” and “luxury” have completely different effects on the price of Airbnb. The word “cozy” seems to be associated with less expensive Airbnb listings than average, while the word “luxury” seems to be associated with more expensive air bnb listings on average.

We can conclude that there is much more to choosing the best NYC Airbnb, then just the reviews you see online. You must take into consideration other variables such as location, price, and the popularity of the place. So always make sure you do your research before renting out that five star Airbnb you think is the best option!
